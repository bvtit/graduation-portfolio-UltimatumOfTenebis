OSHA

by Gabriel Shawn KokEnnen

OSHA 10 and 30 training are the training courses for workers, employers, and supervisors covered in 10-hour and 30-hour duration respectively. The training includes guidelines to recognize, abate, avoid, and prevent safety and health hazards at worksites through record keeping, proper material and machine handling, industrial hygiene, fall protection, and ergonomics.
