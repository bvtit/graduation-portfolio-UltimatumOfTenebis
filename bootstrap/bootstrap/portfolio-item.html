Project Description

The Sophomore Project in Information Technology is the final work of graduating sophomores at Blackstone Valley Tech. H.S. The project is based on everything you have learned this year in your Cisco training, and Project management. It is based on a model of a national corporation that has hired your company to install and set up a new Cisco network in their 6 branches. The Sophomore Project is an opportunity for each student to create a corporate network including the subnet, utilizing business, and project management skills and techniques that have been introduced over the last two years of Information Technology class. The major part of the project will be configuring the subnet for the corporate network. The project will also include researching and pricing the proper equipment needed to build the network. Each student must show a final product of work as this will exhibit the set of competencies outlined in the MA DESE Vocation Frameworks for ISS and Networking, and demonstrates that the student has the ability to perform the work needed to complete the project.
